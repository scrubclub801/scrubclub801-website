About Scrub Club

Cleaning your space like it’s our own.

Scrub Club 801 was created by Nella and Baylee to make local home cleaning feel more personal, organized, and dependable. We focus on what matters most to each client, from everyday upkeep to the details that are easy to miss.

Our approach is simple: listen carefully, communicate clearly, respect every home, and leave each space feeling lighter than we found it.
